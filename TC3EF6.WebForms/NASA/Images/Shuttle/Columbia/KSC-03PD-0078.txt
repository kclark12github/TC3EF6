KSC-03PD-0078 (01/15/2003) --- KENNEDY SPACE CENTER, FLA. -- A view of Space Shuttle Columbia through reedy grasses in nearby water. Columbia is revealed after rollback of the Rotating Service Structure on Launch Pad 39A. Columbia is scheduled for launch Jan. 16 at 10:39 a.m. EST on mission STS-107, a research mission.
