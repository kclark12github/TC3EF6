KSC-03PD-0071 (01/15/2003) --- KENNEDY SPACE CENTER, FLA. -- Cast in late afternoon shadows, Space Shuttle Columbia, with its orange external tank and white solid rocket boosters, is revealed as the Rotating Service Structure rolls back . The Shuttle sits atop the Mobile Launcher Platform. Columbia is scheduled for launch Jan. 16 at 10:39 a.m. EST on mission STS-107..
