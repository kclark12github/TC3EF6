Authentication is based on entries in the Domain Name System (DNS). If your 
computer is in the .mil domain and you are being denied service here, 
it is most likely because your computer is not properly identified as a host 
in a domain name server. 

Please contact your site network manager and have your host added to 
your name server forward and reverse look-up files. If you have any questions 
regarding this, send email to sst@force.cnsl.spear.navy.mil 
or call 804-490-5068.
