CGN-37 Ship's Seal

USS SOUTH CAROLINA (CGN 37)

OFFICIAL CREST

The circular shield on SOUTH CAROLINA's crest depicts the ship's never- ending vigilance and continuity of service to her Navy and country. Below the shield, written in Navy Blue and Gold, is the motto of the State of South Carolina: "Prepared in Mind and Resources."

A bright red outline of the state, superimposed over a gold anchor, represents the ship, which is surrounded by the blue waters over which it reigns. In the right hand portion of the state outline is the symbol of the atom, power source of the ship. The five gold stars spaced across the shield are a reminder that CGN 37 is the fifth ship to bear the name SOUTH CAROLINA. Finally, the overall color scheme of red, white, and blue represents the flag of the nation which SOUTH CAROLINA so proudly serves.
