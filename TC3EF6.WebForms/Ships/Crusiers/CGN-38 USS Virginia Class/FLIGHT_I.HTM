To be eligible for a commercial pilot certificate, a person must-
(a) Be at least 18 years of age;
(b) Be able to read, speak, and understand the english language........
(c) Hold at least a valid second class medical certificate issued under Part 67 of this chapter (FARs).....
(d) Pass a written examination appropriate to the aircraft rating sought.......
(e) Pass an oral and flight test appropriate to the aircraft rating sought.......

FAR 61.129 AIRPLANE RATING: AERONAUTICAL EXPERIENCE

(a) An applicant for a commercial pilot certificate must with an airplane rating must hold a private pilot certificate with an airplane rating.........

(b) Flight time as pilot. An applicant for a commercial pilot certificate with an airplane rating must have a total of at least 250 hours of flight time as pilot.............. The total flight time must include-

Multi-engine Rating


An applicant for an additional class rating must present a logbook record ensorsed by an appropriately rated flight instructor showing that he is competent to operate as pilot in command of an aircraft of the class sought.

Pass an oral and flight test.

A multi-engine rating added to a private pilot certificate usually requires about 10 hours of flight instruction. A private pilot multi-engine rating with instrument privileges requires approximately 15 hours.
