About your Host . . .

So who would take the time to put together a site such as this, you ask?
My name is Joel Huston and I served as an officer in the Engineering Department from
October 3rd, 1991 to the end, November 10th, 1994. I held positions as nub officer (five months), M-1 Division Officer (11 months), A Division Officer (12 months), and D.A.V.E. (8 months). If you need to know what D.A.V.E. stands for, drop me an email.

Presently, I work for for Applied Materials in Santa Clara, CA. Applied is the world's largest maker of semiconductor manufacturing equipment and I'm glad to work there (even more so since I survived some recent layoffs). In my spare time I maintain this web site and help Mike and Carol Bleyle remodel the house they just bought in Fremont, CA.

Why spend all the time to maintain this site? First, it's not alot of time (I'm single, so I suppose that's relative) and HTML is real easy. Second, I have good memories of my time on Virginia and I like to look back. One of my favorite memories is ORSE '93 (no, really): while doing the FRSU after a feed rupture, I looked over at throttleman's panel and saw all three TG vacuum gages going up in unison; what a great watch team, I thought. With the ORSE team CO in there, all I could do was break a little smile. Can't believe we got an average on that drill. Sure there were also some (very) bad times, but those memories have decayed away as fast as N-16 (sorry, couldn't resist a little nuc lingo there). I guess not standing duty for 2 years and getting to go home every night can do that.

So anyway, I hope you enjoy the site. If you have any suggestions or comments, please drop me an email at huston@netgate.net.
