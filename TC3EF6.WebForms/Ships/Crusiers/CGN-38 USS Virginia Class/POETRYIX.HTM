Prologue

I want to extend thanks and credit to my friend and fellow poet David Bolduc. It was he who encouraged me and who has been a constant help and constructive critic of my work, sometimes chiding me, sometimes giving me hell for being sloppy.

Though our styles and subjects are at times greatly disparate, his is some of the best work there is to read. David can usually be found on the alt.rec.poems news groups. Just look for the initials DB following the title.

I hope you enjoy the works you read here, and that they stir your conscience and emotions. Please feel free to drop me a line and let me know what you think.

- Sean McIntyre
24 Sept 96
