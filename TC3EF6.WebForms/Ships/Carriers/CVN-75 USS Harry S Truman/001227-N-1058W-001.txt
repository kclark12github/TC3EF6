Port Said, Egypt, Dec. 27, 2000 —As seen through a night-vision lens, USS Harry S. Truman (CVN 75) begins her transit through the Suez Canal. Truman, the Navy's newest nuclear-powered aircraft carrier, is enroute to the Arabian Gulf to support Operation Southern Watch. U.S. Navy photo by Photographer's Mate 3rd Class H. Dwain Willis.
