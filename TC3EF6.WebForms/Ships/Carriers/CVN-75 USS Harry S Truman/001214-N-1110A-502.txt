The Mediterranean Sea, Dec. 14 , 2000 — James Brown, Terry Bradshaw and Howie Long check out a specially painted "Fox" F/A-18 Hornet to be used for the taping of the network's NFL Pre-Game Show aboard USS Harry S. Truman (CVN 75). Bradshaw, Long, Brown and Cris Collinsworth will air their NFL pre-game show from the flight deck of Truman. Truman is on its maiden six-month deployment to the Mediterranean Sea and Arabian Gulf. U. S. Navy photo by Photographer's Mate 1st Class Tina M. Ackerman. [001214-N-1110A-502] Dec. 14, 2000. Hi-Rez.
