The Mediterranean Sea, Dec. 16, 2000 — The Dallas Cowboy Cheerleaders perform on the flight deck for the crew of USS Harry S Truman (CVN 75) during the taping of the Fox NFL Pre-game Show. They later were part of an hour-long USO show in the carrier's hangar. Truman is on its maiden six-month deployment to the Mediterranean Sea and Arabian Gulf. U. S.
