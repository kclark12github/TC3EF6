History

Authorized 4 March 1917, MONTANA (BB-51) was laid down 1 September 1920; but construction of the SOUTH DAKOTA-class battleship by Mare Island Navy Yard, Vallejo, Calif., was canceled 8 February 1922 by the Washington Treaty on the limitation of naval armament. MONTANA was struck from the Navy list 24 August 1923 and sold 25 October 1923 to be scrapped.
