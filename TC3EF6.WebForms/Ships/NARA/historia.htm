Index of Resources for Historians

This Index is maintained jointly by the Department of History of the University of Kansas and the Lehrstuhl für Ältere deutsche Literaturwissenschaft der Universität Regensburg, and is managed by Eric Marzo (eric.marzo@rrzc1.rz.uni-regensburg.de) of Regensburg and Lynn Nelson (lhnelson@raven.cc.ukans.edu) of Kansas. Please direct your notices of new URL's or inoperative links to whichever of the managers is more convenient to you, or to this site. The following index consists of a single large file (approximately 215 Kb) offering over 1900 connections arranged alphabetically by subject and name.

Given the large number of sites of interest to historians, this index does not pretend to be exhaustive although we attempt to include as many sites as come to our attention. For reasons of security, however, telnet connections are not included. Our general aim is to offer a sufficient number and variety of pointers to suggest the wealth of material available, to allow the user to build a personal bookmark file quickly and effectively, and to provide those building web sites a convenient source of pointers.
