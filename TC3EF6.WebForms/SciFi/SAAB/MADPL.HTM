Phil LaMarr's film credits include Strange Days,  It's Pat  and Dead Presidents. But it was in Pulp Fiction  that movie-goers got to see a whole different side of LaMarr - the inside. Yes that was Phil's brain that John Travolta accidentally splattered against the rear window of Samuel L. Jackson's car.

On television, Phil's appeared in Living Single,  The George Carlin Show,  Hangin' with Mr. Cooper,  The Second Half,  Mad About You,  The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air  and Murphy Brown.

Phil has also performed with L.A.'s Groundlings Theater Company and Chicago's Improv Olympic.

MAD's not content just to pick on TV and movies. Sometimes you find the goofiest people in real life. Watch Phil skewer the UBS delivery guy who's got a whole lot of energy but not a whole lot of smarts.
