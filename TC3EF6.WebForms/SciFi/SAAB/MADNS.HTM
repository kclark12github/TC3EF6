With a mother named Madonna (an antique dealer) and a father named Ed Sullivan (a New York state assemblyman), Nicole was destined to go into show business. Growing up in Manhattan, she performed on- and off-Broadway with the First All Children's Theater. Sullivan attended the British American Dramatic Academy and Northwestern University, where she prepared for her future on MAD TV by studying Greek tragedy as well as the works of Shakespeare, Chekhov and Ibsen. A smart cookie, Nicole graduated Northwestern on the Dean's List.

Nicole's television credits include Madman of the People, Party Of Five, Herman's Head,  Diagnosis Murder  and The Golden Girls.
