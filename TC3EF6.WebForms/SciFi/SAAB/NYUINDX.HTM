Drugs, gangs, prostitution, murder - the working conditions stink, but each morning a squad of determined, young cops return to their grim task of bringing justice to the city's streets. Working undercover to expose corruption, bring down drug bosses and bust car thieves, they're New York's finest facing the worst the city has to offer.
It's easy to lose your humanity in this job, but NEW YORK UNDERCOVER also focuses on the real people behind the badges. There's J.C. Williams (Malik Yoba), a jaded detective, streetwise beyond his years; Eddie Torres (Michael DeLorenzo), Williams' hot- tempered partner; Nina Moreno (Lauren Velez), a newcomer to the precinct; and their boss, Lt. Virginia Cooper (Patti D'Arbanville-Quinn), a tough veteran who keeps her charges in line.
NEW YORK UNDERCOVER is the most gripping police drama you've ever seen, set to a contemporary hip-hop beat and filmed in a gritty, compelling style. Executive-produced by Dick Wolf (Miami Vice, Law & Order), each week NEW YORK UNDERCOVER takes you on the streets and puts the action, tension and danger of police work in your face!
