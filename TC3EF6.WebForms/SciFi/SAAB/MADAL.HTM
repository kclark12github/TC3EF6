Coming from a show business family (his dad installed TV antennas in Union, NJ), it was only natural that Artie became an actor. After dropping out of college to study acting with Sandy Dennis and, after dropping out of acting school to earn money as a longshoreman, Artie made his way to New York, where he performed stand-up and helped found the sketch comedy group Live on Tape.

Artie's been seen in Spike Lee's Jungle Fever  as well as the feature Too Pure.

You've seen him in the stands; you've seen him on TV. Now years of intense rooting have taken their toll on the world's most die-hard sports fan, as portrayed by Artie Lange. But can anything prepare him for the doctor's diagnosis?
