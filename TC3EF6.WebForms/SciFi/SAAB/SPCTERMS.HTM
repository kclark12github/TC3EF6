Malaba Day Nonsensical expression. "The day that never comes."
Micks and Mikes Slang for minutes.

Tick Slang for alien.
Turn 'em & Burn 'em Turn around.
Yankee Search A "Yankee Search" is a submarine term for an active sonar ping at the highest level of intensity...it's always detected by an enemy sub because you can hear the darn thing through the hull (and is it ever spooky)...it's implication is that you don't care whether they hear you or not either because you're about to get wacked anyway or you've got them right where you want them...
Wormhole A shortcut from one region of space to another.
Zoomey Slang for a Hammerhead attack jet.
