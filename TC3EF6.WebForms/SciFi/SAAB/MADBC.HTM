Talk about your frequent flyers, by the time Bryan moved to the United States at age 14, he'd lived in The Philippines, India, Lebanon, Pakistan, Greece and Saudi Arabia.

After graduating American University with a degree in history, Bryan complied with his parents' request that he give up gainful employment and pursue a career in stand-up comedy and acting. The rest, as they say, is history. Callen has performed in several stage plays and independent films as well as appearing at the prestigious "Comic Strip" and "Stand Up New York" comedy clubs.

A talented impressionist in the tradition of Dana Carvey and Martin Short, check out Bryan's dead-on impersonation of Seinfeld's Kramer in MAD's parody, "Clintfeld."
