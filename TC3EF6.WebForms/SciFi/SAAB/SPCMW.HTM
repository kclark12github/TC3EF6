MORGAN WEISSER

Award-winner Morgan's television credits include a starring role in the movies "Extreme Closeup," directed by Peter Horton, and "Long Road Home" and the mini-series "Stay the Night," with Barbara Hershey and Jane Alexander. He also guest starred on the series "Crime and Punishment," "China Beach" and "Law & Order."

His feature film credits include starring roles in "Mother," with Diane Ladd and Olympia Dukakis, "Shortcut to Paradise," "Prayer of the Rollerboys" and "City Limits."

Also a veteran of the theater, Morgan's stage appearances include "Being at Home with Claude," for which he won the L.A. Drama Critics Circle Award for Best Performance, "The Coyote Cycle" and "Interbellum."

Morgan was born May 12 in Los Angeles. His father is actor Norbert Weisser ("Schindler's List").

On SPACE: ABOVE AND BEYOND, Morgan portrays Nathan West.
