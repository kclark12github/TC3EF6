NEVER NO MORE

(SE-115)

WITH THE WAR ESCALATING, EARTH'S FORCES GO UP AGAINST A 'SUPER-KILLER'; VANSEN REUNITES WITH A FORMER LOVER

Part 1 of 2

In part one of a two-part story, the war is escalating as Earth's forces assemble for an all-out attack against an advanced Alien fighter ship. Meanwhile, in the midst of this heightened alert, Shane Vansen is reunited with a fighter pilot she almost married.
