LEVEL OF NECESSITY

(SE-114)

A 'DARK' BRANCH OF THE MILITARY TRIES TO RECRUIT DAMPHOUSSE INTO A SPECIAL SQUAD

In a story based on a phenomenon reported in memoirs from soldiers in the Vietnam War, Damphousse begins having premonitions in the heat of battle and saves the 58th Squadron from destruction. But a "dark" branch of the military wants to recruit her into a special squad to capitalize on her psychic abilities for covert operations.
