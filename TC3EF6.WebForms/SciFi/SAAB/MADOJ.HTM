Orlando was in show business for five years before he finally got up the nerve to stand in front of the camera. Beginning as a staff writer and then story editor of A Different World,  he moved on to become an executive story editor for the FOX series Roc  and then a co-producer of Sinbad.

Jones' on-screen debut came in 1995 as host of the cable series Sound fX  for the fX Channel.

Only MAD TV would cross the corny Forrest Gump  with the bloody Pulp Fiction. The result, Gump Fiction. Here Orlando deftly plays an hysterical hybrid of the affable Bubba and Samuel L. Jackson's cold-blooded assassin.
