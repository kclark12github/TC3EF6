TYRUS CASSIUS (TC) MCQUEEN

RANK: Lieutenant Colonel, 58th Squadron, US Marine Corps.
RACE: In Vitro
AGE: 38
HOMETOWN: Anchorage, AK
WILDCARD CALL SIGN: Queen Six
BATCH: Kappa 9757, Anchorage Facility, Gene Pool 13C
PLAYED BY: James Morrison

Nicknamed "Black Knight," McQueen is a battle veteran and commander of the 58th Squadron. Prior to leading the 58th, McQueen was in charge of the 127th Airborne (also called the 127th Attack Wing and the 127th Squadron), The Angry Angels. During one of the first battles of the Alien War, the 127th was decimated, leaving McQueen with burns over 20% of his body. Wise, tough and mysterious McQueen silently bonds with Cooper Hawkes as they watch a fight between the Angry Angels and the Marine recruits at Asteroid bar. Unlike the rebellious Cooper, however, McQueen has built a strong military career, displayed by his cool discipline and taciturn demeanor.
