TOMBSTONE
Tuesday September 17, 1996 - 8:00PM
EASTERN/PACIFIC

Kurt Russell ("Stargate" as the legendary Wyatt Earp and Val Kilmer ("Batman Forever") as Doc Holliday head an all-star cast in the action-paced drama TOMBSTONE, a world broadcast premiere presentation. Bill Paxton ("Twister") and Sam Elliott ("Buffalo Girls") play Earp’s brothers, Morgan and Virgil. Also starring are Michael Biehn ("The Rock"), Powers Boothe ("Nixon"), Robert Burke ("Rambling Rose"), Thomas Haden Church ("Ned & Stacey"), Dana Delany ("China Beach"), Stephen Lang ("Crime Story"), Joanna Pacula ("Gorky Park"), Jason Priestly ("Beverly Hills, 90210"), Michael Rooker ("Cliffhanger"), Jon Tenney ("Good Company"), Billy Zane ("The Phantom") and Charlton Heston (Academy Award winner for "Ben Hur"). Robert Mitchum narrates.
