SHANE VANSEN

RANK: Captain, 58th Squadron, US Marine Corps.
RACE: White
AGE: 22
HOMETOWN: San Diego, CA
WILDCARD CALL SIGN: Queen of Diamonds
SERIAL NUMBER: 560-51-03318
PLAYED BY: Kristen Cloke

As children, Shane and her younger sisters watched from the attic as Artificial Intelligence Rebels (human-like cyborgs originally built to serve humankind) broke into the Vansen home and executed her parents. In her own words, Shane explained why she joined the Marines, "I joined for me... my life. I don't want to take care of anyone for awhile." Shane's hatred and fear of the Al's, or Silicates, is the driving force in her life.
