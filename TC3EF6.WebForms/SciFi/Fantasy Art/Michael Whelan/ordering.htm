UNITED STATES SHIPPING

When possible, we ship UPS-- please be sure to give us your street address. If you prefer the U.S. Mail or to ship to a different address, please make a note of it.

The shipping charge is determined by the letter in parentheses following the price: i.e. $15 (F). If all your items have the same letter you just pay the one fee. Please use the chart below or on the Order Form.

If your order appears damaged, refuse it and UPS will return it to us for a replacement. The shipping fee covers the cost of the insurance and replacement, but we have to file the claim, you just arrange for UPS to return it to us.

Please allow up to 4 weeks for delivery, longer if you pay by check. You can hurry your order by charging it or sending a money order. Air delivery is available.

To charge your order by phone, call (203) 798-6063 Monday through Saturday 10 am-5 pm EST.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

We can ship your order via Air Mail, United Parcel Service (UPS), or Federal Express (FedEx). Surface Shipping is available but not recommended. Air Mail packages can take 4 weeks to reach some parts of the world. UPS and FedEx shipments take 1 week or less.

Fees do not include duty or taxes. Try to learn your country's customs rules about art reproductions. Signed prints may be considered the same as original art and not subject to duty; be sure to pay only what is necessary.

Please use VISA, MasterCard, or American Express, and specify which service to use. We will find the lowest rate and charge it to your card. If you want to send an International Money Order, please use the chart below as a guide.

  There is a custom framing shop here at Glass Onion, so be assured that all our matting and framing is of a very high quality. We use acid-free tape when matting all prints and posters, and on limited edition or signed prints, we use only acid-free mats and backing boards.

Any print from our catalogs can be framed in your choice of 12 anodized metal frames and plexiglass. We also have many wood mouldings in natural and stained finishes as well as gold and silver leaf ornate frames. If you prefer one of those, please call for information. All framing prices include shipping for U.S. orders!
