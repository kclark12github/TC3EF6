Dragonsbane

 

From Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder...

I loved this book-- the characters of the protagonists and the dragon are very engaging and held my interest right from the start. A narrative approach seemed best for this cover, and I chose this particular scene because of the uncertainty of the relationship between the woman and the dragon. Is he saving her or about to dine on her? That kind of uncertainty draws the reader in and entices him or her to buy the book to find out what is going on.

Also, I hadn't done a painting with a castle in it for years and I couldn't pass up the chance!
