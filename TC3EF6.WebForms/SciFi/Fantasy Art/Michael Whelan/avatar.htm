Passage: The Avatar

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

This painting is the first of my Passage series. The image came to me while I was experiencing a "waking dream," floating in a sensory deprivation tank in New York City.

It seems odd that though the float was such a positive and fruitful experience for me, I haven't taken the time since to repeat it. I suppose it's because I've had a  flood of concepts to develop since then, and I feel no desire at this time to search for more. I'll be lucky if I can manage to realize the ones I've already come up with.
