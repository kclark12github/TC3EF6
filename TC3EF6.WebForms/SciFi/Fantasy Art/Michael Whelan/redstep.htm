Passage: The Red Step

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

I was probably clinically depressed when I painted this. Stymied by the first serious artistic block in my illustration career, I felt helpless to do anything but fret for a couple of weeks. Eventually I realized I had to use my art as a way to climb out of the mood I was in. By devoting my time to this personal project, I found my way out of the hole, and when I went back to the assignment I was able to find the answer to the problem and move on.
