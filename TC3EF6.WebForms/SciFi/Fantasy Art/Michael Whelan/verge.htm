Passage: Verge

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

There are other, more metaphysical meanings to be derived from this painting, but at the time I was doing it I had decided to "step off the brink," professionally speaking. That is, I had made up my mind to ignore any lingering feelings of self-doubt and commit myself to a lengthy period of entirely personal work, no commissions.
