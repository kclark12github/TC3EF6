Santiago

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

For the cover of the novel Santiago, by Mike Resnick, the essential challenge was to give the ship behind the central character a sentient, living quality. I was thinking of insects, especially fleas and bees, when I conceived the shape of the thing. The window in front of the ship is deliberated colored and shaped to recall the eye of a bee.
