Second Foundation

 

From Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder...

Having passed through terrible destruction, the former hub of the Galaxy is now a pastoral world, save for the occasional towering reminder of its past. Arkady stands on the edge of a high metallic ruin overlooking the new Trantor. The last remnants of the long-lost empire rise to form the shape of an archway leading through the lessons of the past to a new, bright future. (The shape is also echoed in Arkady's legs.)

Natural forces have regained dominance. The beautiful blue sky has a few clouds signifying the renewed freedom of weather no longer controlled by mankind, and native life forms attack the remaining metal.
