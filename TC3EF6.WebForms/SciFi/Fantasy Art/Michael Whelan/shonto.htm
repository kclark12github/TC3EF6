Shonto's Garden

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

Since Sean Russell, the author of The Initiate Brother, draws from both Chinese and Japanese lore for the oriental fantasy this painting illustrates, I made sure to reflect the same sources in the two banners behind the monk. One is of a Chinese motif, the other Japanese.

This is one painting that made me thank the kind souls who invented the airbrush. There is no better tool available for achieving the sort of misty effects seen in the background of this work.
