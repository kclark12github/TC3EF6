Climber

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

This painting follows The Red Step both chronologically and thematically. On the day I finished it I brought the painting to Audrey's frame shop to show it to her. She was busy with a client on a frame job, so I left the painting on a table and went to get some lunch. When I cam back I was rewarded with the sight of the client running her hand over the upper third of the painting to feel the texture of the surface... which, of course, isn't real but painted.

We painters live for such moments!
