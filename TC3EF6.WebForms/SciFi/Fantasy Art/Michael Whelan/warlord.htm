The Warlord of Mars

From Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder...

Since Barsoom is a desert planet, it seemed likely that most of my cover art would feature scenes in arid locales. To avoid the overuse of backgrounds of that nature I seized the opportunity to depict a scene with John Carter in a forest. Here he is accompanied by Woola the calot, a Barsoomian "watchdog" of great loyalty and legendary ferocity.
