Delirium's Mistress

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

Tanith Lee is such a visual writer of fantasy that she gives an artist an inspiring painting concept on nearly every page. She offers an inexhaustible supply of fascinating ideas.

Tanith must tap into some well of Jungian subconscious symbolism in her writing. Her stories carry an intriguing weight of significance even though they are "only" fantasies. Something of the same sense of connection can be found also in works by H.P. Lovecraft and David Lindsay, among others.
