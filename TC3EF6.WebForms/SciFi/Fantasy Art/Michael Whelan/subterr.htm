Subterraneans

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

Companion piece to Climber and The Red Step, this piece is firmly set within my Fading Star series.

My original conception of the image didn't seem right somehow, and I had been wondering how I could improve on it. While attending my daughter's winter music concert at her school, the answer came to me and I could see it in my mind's eye. Fortunately I had a little pad of paper and a pencil in my pocket on which to sketch it while it was still fresh in my mind.

The perspective is false. For some reason whenever I tried to make the picture do what I wanted it to do via the conventional laws of perspective, it lost something vital, the feeling of curving immensity going on forever. So I chucked what I've learned about true perspective and painted it the way it felt right.
