A Fighting Man of Mars

From Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder...

Since I was endeavoring to be the illustrator most faithful to Burroughs' descriptions, Lester del Rey and I had a lengthy discussion about whether the white lizard I wanted to show had six or eight legs. The Barsoomian counterparts to four-legged creatures on Earth always had eight legs, but Burroughs specifically described the white lizard as having only six. We could not decide if this was his mistake, or if Burroughs intended to have six legs because it had spent its life in isolation in the cave. But the author wrote six, so I painted six.
