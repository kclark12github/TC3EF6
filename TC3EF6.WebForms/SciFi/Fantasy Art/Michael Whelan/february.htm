And don't forget...

Michael Whelan's only major art exhibition of 1997 will be taking place August 30 at a Cape Cod regional art gallery called Tree's Place. This will be Michael's first major exhibition of his uncommissioned fine art, so believe me when I say it's going to be a special occasion. Anyone interested in details can check out their web site, or ask Julian Baird personally.

The initial response to the exhibition has been very strong,
so please plan ahead accordingly if you're interested in attending.
