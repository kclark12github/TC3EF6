Little Fuzzy

 

From Wonderworks...

No, this is not my bid for the title of "the Charles Keene of Science Fiction." But how else could I portray furry little aliens who are described as being "irresistibly cute?" I've never met nor seen a picture of H. Beam Piper, but I've been told that the way I depicted the hero Pappy Jack bears some resemblance to him.
