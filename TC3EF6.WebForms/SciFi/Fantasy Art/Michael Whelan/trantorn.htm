Trantorian Dream

...from Foundation's Edge

 

From Michael Whelan's
Works of Wonder...

Above the dreary Spectacle of Trantor reduced to rubble, the protagonist gazes at the enigma of the Galaxy and attempts to divine its future course. (The scene is actually a dream vision, as one could not be on Trantor viewing the Galaxy of which it is a part.)

To created the jumbled, trash-heap surface of the planet, I experimented with welding plastic model parts together and using them as airbrush masks. The whole experience only served to remind me of why I rarely bother with model building, costume sewing, etc. The plastic pieces weren't flat enough to act as a mask over the board and they just soaked up the paint. I ended up painting the details out of my head anyway!
