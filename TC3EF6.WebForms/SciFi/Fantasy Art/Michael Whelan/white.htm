The White Dragon

 

From Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder...

Although this is the third book in the series, this was my first Pern cover, and I was determined to create the dragons as faithfully to Anne McCaffrey's description as possible. I studied crocodiles and bat wings, and wrote to Anne with many questions. After all that, it was somewhat of a let-down to have Judy-Lynn Del Rey tell me that based on their descriptions, it would be physically impossible for the dragons to fly. It's because they "think" they can that they do.

This painting represents a major benchmark in my career as an artist. It was my first painting to appear on a hard cover (and a best-selling on, at that) and seemed to bring me a small measure of national recognition. It has been a successful print, and the original has been popular in shows all over the country. I guess I couldn't miss with red-white-and-blue as a color scheme!
