Buffo

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

Reading a novel by M. John Harrison always leaves me with the feeling that I've experienced some vivid, chaotic dream full of important but indecipherable symbols and images. This painting attempts to take the mood and give it a visual expression. The title is the name of the character wearing the mask.

This is one of the few paintings I've traded for a piece of work by another artist. in this case the other artist was the fantasy sculptor Hap Henriksen. I feel safe in saying that we both believe we got the better of the deal... which is how such things should be.
