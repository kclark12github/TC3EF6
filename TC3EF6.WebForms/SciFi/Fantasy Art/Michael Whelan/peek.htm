Peekaboo Fuzzies

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

They're supposed to be irresistibly cute. No apologies. My son likes it, and it hangs in his room when it's not at shows.

The painting was originally commissioned for the children's version of H. Beam Piper's novel Little Fuzzy-- it decorated the endpapers of the book.
