Dragonquest

 

From Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder...

Chief menace to Pern is "Thread," a corrosive and devastating life form which migrates from the Red Star (seen in the sky of each of the first three covers) to fall on Pern at regular intervals. Only the dragons, with their ability to literally burn the Thread out of the sky before it reaches the ground, can save the inhabitants of Pern. The excitement of the Thread-fighting episodes was an irresistible subject for my second painting in this series.

Unfortunately, I seemed to have made a good. McCaffrey visualized the Thread as falling like straight strands of spaghetti, whereas my concept of it was of squirming ribbon-like things descending in clumps and tangles. It's much too late to correct it now, but I'm left with the problem of what to do if I ever paint another such scene in the future: should I use my own precedent or McCaffrey's text as a guide?
