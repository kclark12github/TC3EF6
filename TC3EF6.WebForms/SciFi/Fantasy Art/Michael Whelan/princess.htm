A Princess of Mars

 

From Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder...

For the first book in the series, it was important to me to do a painting that captured the feeling of the series as a whole: the otherworldliness of Barsoom and its two moons, the valor of its main hero, the weird and colorful life-forms, and the splendor of the civilization.

To accurately portray the Tharks, the green men of Barsoom, I sculpted a model in olive-green Plasticene, using white plastic for the tusks. I so enjoyed working in a three-dimensional form that I kept the model long after the completion of the paintings for the series. In fact I kept it a little too long on a high shelf in my studio where it grew so much brown mold
that when it fell off and broke, I didn't try to repair it.
