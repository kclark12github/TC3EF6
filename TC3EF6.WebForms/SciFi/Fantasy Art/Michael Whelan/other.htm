The Art of Michael Whelan

Limited versions of "coffee table" art books often have edition sizes in the thousands and are accompanied by limited edition prints. Although the books and prints are hand-signed by the artist, they are virtually identical copies. When Michael Whelan decided to produce a special version of his book, he wanted to offer the collector not only a beautifully crated book, but also one that he had worked on with his own hands.

Here is that book in a signed and numbered edition of only 500. Every book is unique-- Michael Whelan has painted each limited edition leaf in different colors and styles. His use of interference pigments assures their originality because they cannot be successfully photographed and reproduced. In effect, there is a hand-tinted print on museum quality deckle-edged paper hand bound into each book.

This extraordinary volume is hand covered in white German Iris Linen with gold stamping and a dust jacket produced exclusively for this edition. It is then secured in a hand bound slipcase. The title and Whelan's distinctive signature are blind debossed in the fine Japanese cloth. This book is sure to be the centerpoint of your collection.
