Chanur's Breakout

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

An opportunity to paint C.J. Cherryh's catlike Hani is always welcome. I am fortunate to have done several covers for the books in this popular series, yet for Chanur's Legacy I came up with more new concepts than ever before. I think the reason is that the changes in the personalities of the characters are so engaging, and the other aliens in the series are great. I haven't had much of a chance to paint them yet, so I hope I get to do at least one more cover before the series draws to a close.

In my initial concept for this image, the eyes of the second Hani glowed in the dark of the lead Hani's shadow. The publisher didn't like the effect because it seemed to draw attention away from the cat in front. I went along with her opinion, but I never felt comfortable with the decision: I made the change by painting the eyes on removable plastic tape, so I have the option of restoring the painting to its original state! I still haven't decided.
