Wide

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

This painting touches on two themes, that of the Passage series and that of a series I call Sanctuary. It suggests a tangent point where conceptual spheres meet and is an offshoot of the painting Open.

It felt so important to me to establish a connection between the two themes that I did two versions of this idea. Now that I've touched on this world, I'm awaiting the chance to realize it further in painted form. I have many notes and sketches complementing my mental impressions of this realm, which feels real to me.
