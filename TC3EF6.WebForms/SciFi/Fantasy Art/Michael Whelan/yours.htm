Yours Truly

 

From Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder...

In 1983, I was commissioned by Del Rey Books and Lucasfilm Ltd. to create a portrait of Yoda which was eventually used on a novelty item called "My Jedi Journal."

Fortunately some b/w photos of Yoda were provided for my reference, though of course, I would have much preferred a studio visit from the Master Jedi himself.
