Gunslinger '88

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

The background of this painting for The Dark Tower, by Stephen King, is a peculiar mustardy color that reflects the dusty, oppressive feeling of the book to me. I also thought it might go well with a gold foil type. At any rate, the art director at the book company didn't agree. He sent the painting to a digital photo retouch lab, and they put a different background not he image: a sky from one of the other Gunslinger paintings I had done previously! So, while the printed cover is all my art, there is no such painting in existence.
