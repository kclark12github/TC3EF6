Thuvia, Maid of Mars

 

From Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder...

Here was another one of the Barsoomian beasts that was basically an Earthly one with more legs. To portray the banth (Barsoomian "lion") in all his fierceness without having him look silly, I curved his body around in such a way as to make his multileggedness less apparent. I also had to be careful in doing Thuvia herself, as everyone on Barsoom goes nude, and yet full-frontal nudity was unacceptable for the cover. By turning her away from the viewer, I thought I had this problem solved, but Lester del Rey had me add the G-string just to be on the safe side. The rest of the painting is in acrylics, but I did that G-string in gouache in case I ever get the urge to wipe it off.
