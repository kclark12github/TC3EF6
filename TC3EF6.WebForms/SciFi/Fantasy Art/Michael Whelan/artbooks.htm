The following represents an archive of paintings featured in past galleries of this site. The organization is based in a table of contents format from each of Michael's art books. Artwork that has yet to be featured in an art book has been collected on the table at the end of the page.

Currently, Michael's first two art books, Wonderworks (available through GoG) and Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder, are Out of Print and rather difficult to find. The Art of Michael Whelan and Something In My Eye are currently in print and available at most finer book stores or from our catalog.

Is there a painting you would like to see but hasn't been featured yet? Send us a request!
