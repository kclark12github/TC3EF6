Sailor on the Seas of Fate

 

From Wonderworks...

Nice things sometimes come out of necessity. My usual dilemma-- keeping the top third of the painting empty enough for type-- was solved by the misty fog that envelopes the scene. The usual riggings, sail, and mast are somewhat obscured and fade away behind it, leaving plenty of room for the type and setting-- exactly the mood that the author created to surround
his mystical anti-hero Elric.
