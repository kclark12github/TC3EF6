The Ultimate Enemy

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

Sometimes the simplest statements have the strongest force. The image is ostensibly a representation of the struggle to preserve life against the onslaught of the Berserker war machines described in The Ultimate Enemy, by Fred Saberhagen. I view the painting in the larger context of the conflict between natural life and the sterility of man-made technology. Which will win out in the end?

I'm betting on the plant. Whether humanity will be around to view the outcome, though, is another question.
