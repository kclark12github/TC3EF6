Elric And The Sinking City

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

Unlike all the other Elric paintings I have done, the cover for Michael Moorcock's Elric At The End Of Time is not an action scene. This time I wished to portray the moody antihero in a more somber and reflective state. My intent was serious, though the author's use of the character in this book is rather cynical and self-mocking. My goal was to portray the Elric in the other books in the series.
