Weyrworld

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

My schedule gets booked up many months in advance, so Del Rey Books commissioned me to do this cover before the book was even written. They outlined the plot of the book (All the Weyrs of Pern) for me, and the author, Anne McCaffrey, gave me a scene she had written which she suggested I use as inspiration for the cover.

Before I started work on the preliminary concepts, I had a dream about this scene. It resembled what you see here except that there were no trees. Anne liked the idea and gave her okay. But since I often second-guess myself, not believing that my first idea can possibly be the best, I did several alternate concepts. Though I had some other good ideas, I kept coming back to the one from my dream.
