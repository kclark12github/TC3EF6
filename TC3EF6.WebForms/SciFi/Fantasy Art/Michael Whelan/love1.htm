Lovecraft's Nightmare Part One

 

Michael Whelan says of the paintings...

The Lovecraft panels were done for Del Rey Books. They wanted 7 HPL novels but only had a budget for two paintings, so I offered to do two paintings worth of horror that they could then dissect as they saw fit for the book covers. When I started on the works it was a beautiful spring May morning (complete with happily chirping birds, etc.) ...so to get in the mood I blacked out all the windows of my studio and put on "X" by Klaus Schulze, cranked up my compressor, and jumped right into the painting.
