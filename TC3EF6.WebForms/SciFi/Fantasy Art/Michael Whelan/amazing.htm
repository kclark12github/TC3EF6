The Amazing Dragon

 

From The Art of Michael Whelan

Amazing Stories magazine commissioned me to do the first cover for a new, modern edition of their publication. I thought that the cover of the premier issue, like the editorial content of the magazine, should encompass both the realms of science fiction and fantasy. In keeping with this theme, I selected the astronaut as a symbol for science fiction and the dragon for fantasy.

As for my personal creative impulse: I'd been wanting an excuse to paint a black dragon ever since I did The White Dragon.
