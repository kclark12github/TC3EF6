John Carter of Mars

 

From Michael Whelan's Works of Wonder...

John Carter and Deja Thoris ride off into the sunset! All kidding aside, I wanted to wrap up the series with a painting that evoked the romantic notion of the deathless hero and the woman of his dreams winging off to further adventures against the backdrop of the full majesty of their planet.
