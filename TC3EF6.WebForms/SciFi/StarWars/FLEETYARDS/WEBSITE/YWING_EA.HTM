Y-Wing Training Mission - Easy



This mission will effectively use the powerful weapons mounted on the Y-Wing. The Y-Wings lack of speed will be neutralized with equally slow opponents.

On it's way to the Outer Rim, the Freedom has encountered an Imperial convoy. Four Corellian Corvettes are escorting a flotila of twelve bulk freighters. Intelligence reports that the freighters may be carrying supplies of war. The capture of these supplies would not only hurt the Empire, but would also aid in the Freedom's mission.

The convoy is making a short stop at a nearby outpost to refuel. You, Gold Leader, have been ordered to jump in ten minutes ahead of the Freedom. Destroy any war ships in the area. Identify all freighters and containers, then disable them for pickup by the Freedom. Once the area has been secured, fall into a holding pattern and await the Freedom. There should be no snags in this mission, but good luck anyways, and may the Force(TM) be with you.
