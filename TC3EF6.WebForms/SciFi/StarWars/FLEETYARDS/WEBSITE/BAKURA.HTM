Bakura

Following the destruction of the Death Star mark II, the Alliance Fleet orbiting Endor discovered a message container meant for the late Emperor. It was a call for help originating from the remote system of Bakura. The Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Flurry w as dispatched, with Luke Skywalker in command, to aid the Imperials in that system, who were under attack by an unknown race.
