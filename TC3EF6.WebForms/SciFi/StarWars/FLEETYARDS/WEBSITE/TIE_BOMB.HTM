New Republic Primary Fleetyards

TIE Bomber

The TIE Bomber is the most heavily armed and armored of the older generation of TIEs. Specifically designed to deliver as much firepower as possible, the Bomber is armed with over 8 torpedoes. Heavily armored, it can withstand several volleys of laser fire from nearly any starfighter out there. However, all of its weapons and armor make it an extreamly slow ship. Subsequently, its top speed make it perfect for target practice by most Republic starfighters. But be warned, it may be difficult to stop a large flight of these ships.
