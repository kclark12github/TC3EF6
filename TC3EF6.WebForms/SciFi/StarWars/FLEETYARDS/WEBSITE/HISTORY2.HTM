Historic Combat Mission 2: Raid On Coruscant Docks



Following the Battle of Endor, the Alliance sought to quickly knock the Empire off balance. One move towards this goal was the taking of Coruscant, the heart of the Empire. In order to take Coruscant, which was heavily defended, multiple raids were first carried out.

The most important of these raids was an attack on the Coruscant Fleet Yards. This instellation serviced the Imperial Home Fleet, which defended Coruscant. Without it, the Fleet would have to go nearly twenty light-years from Coruscant to refuel, rearm, and recieve repairs.

Three attacks on neighboring systems were carried out to lure away this instellations defenses. Then, one squadron of X-Wings from Fire Lance Squadron was sent in for a fast strike. An Imperial Fleet had arrived just as the docks were being finished off, and the X-Wings escaped. You will be Fire Lance Leader. Jump in and smash the repair docks, as well as any Imperial craft there. Watch out for the mine field surrounding the docks. A Star Destroyer force will also jump in, so make this a fast kill. Good luck.
