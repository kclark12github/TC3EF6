Academy Simulator

New Republic Academy - Simulator



Flight instructors at the Training Center are in the process of creating training missions for each of the three primary fighters in the New Republic Fleet, the X-Wing, A-Wing, and Y-Wing. Missions for the B-Wing will follow later.

In production, for each figher, are two missions. The first mission is well suited for each particular fighter. In the second mission, you will be forced to use each fighter for what it was never meant to do. Thus, the second mission will be far more challenging. At a later time, an impossible mission will be added, just for fun. Good luck!

Click here for instructions on how to install these missions.

Note: All missions marked with a * are ready to fly.

Note II: Some of these missions are not fair. Some of them may also be impossible. If you find that it is, please mail me and we'll fix it. Thanx.
