The advanced scouts from Blue Squadron have discovered an Imperial supply convoy supplying a secret base in this sector. First, A-Wings from Blue Squadron will jump in and surpress enemy fighters in the area. Next, X-Wings from Red Squadron, led by you, will arrive and secure the area. Red Squadron will provide cover during their attack run and Blue Squadron will hold a defense perimeter, in case something... unexpected jumps in.

This mission is going to be one hell of a dogfight. I hope you're up to it.
