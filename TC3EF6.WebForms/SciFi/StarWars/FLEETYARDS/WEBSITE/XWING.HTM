X-Wing

Perhaps one of the oldest and sturdiest fighters still in use, the X-Wing was the mainstay of the Alliance figher force during the height of the Alliance/Empire war. Armed with four laser cannons and a pair of torpedo tubes, it is the perfect balance between shields, armor, speed, and weapons. Equipped with a hyperdrive, it can be used as a deep-space long-range attack fighter. With the ability to dogfight, bomb, and defend, the X-Wing is still an excellent starfighter.
