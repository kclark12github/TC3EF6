New Republic Academy

A-Wing Training Mission - Hard



This mission will be the greatest nightmare for any A-Wing pilot. The thin shields and light weaponry of the A-Wing will now be put to the test.

Following the discovery of a massive Imperial Fleet orbiting the planet Latus, the Freedom launched all of its fighters in a full forced assult. The A-Wings were held in a point-defense grid near the Freedom. The remaining X-Wings and Y-Wings were sent in, and completely destroyed. Now, the twelve A-Wings of Blue Squadron are all that stands between the Freedom and an Imperial task force.

Your job is to hold the squadron together and fend off the approching TIE's. Before begin destroyed, the X-Wings were able to eliminate a good number of TIE Fighters and TIE Inteceptors, leaving only the Bombers for you to deal with. Dispatch with the Bombers quickly, then head out to delay the approching Imperial ships. Sensors have detected a flight of 12 Corellian Corvettes, with a pair of Destroyers running behind them. The Freedom will be able to outrun the Destroyers, but you will have to deal with the Corvettes before they close with the Freedom. Once the Freedom jumps to safety, re-form your squadron and proceed to the rendevouze point to be picked up. The Freedom is depending on you. Good luck, and may the Force(TM) be with you.

Note: There are reports that the Imperial Commander operating in this region aquired the design specs for the TIE Advanced. Be warned, these fighters may appear, launching from the trailing Star Destroyers. Stay on your guard.
