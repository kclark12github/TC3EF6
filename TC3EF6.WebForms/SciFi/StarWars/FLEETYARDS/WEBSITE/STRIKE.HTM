Strike Cruiser

These fast, relatively strong vessels were never intended to be Main Assault units, but still play an important support role. Noting the large number of weapons and tractor beams, but sparse fighter protection, it is easy to tell that this is a combat starship, and not a carrier. In combat, its tough armor and small size (this thing is really thin) make it a formidable opponent.

----------------

Type: Strike-Class Medium Criuser

Length: 450 meters

Weapons: 30 Type XV Turbolasers

10 SW-5 Ion Cannons

5+ Tractor Beams

Craft: 1 squadron of light fighters (either A-Wing or TIE Fighter)
