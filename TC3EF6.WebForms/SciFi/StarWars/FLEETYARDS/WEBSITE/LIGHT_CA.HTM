Light Mon Calamari Star Cruiser

An idea that sprund into the minds of those ever-busy Mon Calamari scientists was "Why not build a ship that is stronger than a frigate, but still easily built?" Their answer was the MC70 class. These ships are very fast, and well armed for their size, but do not sport heavy shielding or armor. It usually relies on it 4 fighter wings to help it out of any sticky situation it lands in. Still, it is a midsized cruiser and fills a class that was empty for a long time.
