New Republic Academy

X-Wing Training Mission - Hard



This mission is hell. Since the X-Wing has no major weaknesses, unlike other Republic fighters, this mission will simply bury you with enemy ships...

Following the destruction of the local Imperial base, a large Imperial fleet dropped out of hyperspace directly behind the Freedom. A flight of TIE Bombers were able to damage the launch bay(s) on the Freedom, rendering it helpless. Twelve X-Wings from Red Squadron, with you in the lead, are all the fighter cover that the Freedom has now. Since the route back to Republic space has been closed off, the captain has decided to continue with the Freedom's mission to the Outer Rim. You are to hold off all enemy ships long enough for the Freedom to escape, then jump to safety to be picked up. Good luck, and may the Force(TM) be with you.
