Interdictor Cruiser

One of the Empire's most ingenious inventions, the Interdictor Cruiser uses to shell of a old Victory Star Destroyer. With four gravity well projectors, it can serve two roles. First, it prevents any ships nearby from entering hyperspase. Second, any ships traveling through hyperspace will be dragged out automatically because the navigation systems will believe that the vessel is close to a planet. Due to this unique ability, Interdictors are used in many ambush operations and were a favorite tool of Grand Admiral Thrawn.
