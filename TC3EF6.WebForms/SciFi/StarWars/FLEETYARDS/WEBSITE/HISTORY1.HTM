Historic Combat Mission 1: Battle of Sluis Van



Nearly five years after the Battle of Endor, the last of the Empire's warlords rallied the Fleet and drove the Republic back. One of the opening steps in this Grand Admiral's campaigns was to capture Republic capital ships for use. This was accomplished with an attack on Sluis Van.

By hitting nearby systems, as well as Republic shipping lanes, the Adrmial was able to force Mon Calamari Star Cruisers into use as freighters. Using mole miners stolen from Nkllon, as well as a cloaking device, nearly forty capital ships were taken by the Empire. Only last second heroics by Rouge Squadron and the Millenium Falcon kept the valuable ships from falling into Imperial hands.

Here, you will take on the role as Rouge 2. Defend the Fleet from the Imperial assult. Remember, the safety of the Cruisers is your top priority, but you also need to keep the Sluis Van ship yards alive. Good luck.
