TIE Interceptor



The TIE Interceptor is the Imperial counter-part to the A-Wing. It is faster and more maneuverable than the TIE Fighter, as well as more heavily armed and armored. This was the first improvement to the original TIE design in a long time. However, the radical advancement of technology has shown the Empire's ability to advance weapons designs quickly and effectively.
