Battle of Obroa-Skai

This was the first battle where we saw Grand Admiral Thrawn in action. Following a data dump from the Obroa-Skai database, a scout wing from the ISD Chimera headed back. They were followed by four New Republic Assault Frigates and three squadrons of X-Wings. Thrawn then executed a Marg Sabl, completely destroying the New Republic force. Here's how it looked.

As you can see, the Republic Forces are in a miserable position, trapped between four separate Imperial forces. Subsequently, they were utterly annihilated. This was the beginning of Thrawn's campaign to restore the Empire, a campaign which came very close to succeeding...
