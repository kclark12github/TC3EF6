The New Republic Primary Fleet Yards

B-Wing

The B-Wing is the heavy assult figher/bomber of the Republic. Designed by Admiral Ackbar, with help from the master shipbuilders of Slayn & Korpil, it is mounted with heavylasers and a rotating cockpit. It destroyed a large number of TIE's at the Battle of Endor.

-----------------

Class: B-Wing Heavy Starfighter

Length: 20 meters

Speed: 90 MGLT

Weapons: 3 Gyrhil R-9X Heavy Laser Cannons

3 ArMek SW-7a Ion Cannons

2 Krupx MG9 Proton Torpedo launchers (6 torpedoes each)

Shields: 125 SBD

Armor: 60 RU

Special: Rotating Cockpit, S-Foils allowing Atmospheric flight
