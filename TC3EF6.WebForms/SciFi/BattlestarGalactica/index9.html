Battlestar Galactica Images

These are images which were scanned from a photonovel (probably more than one) by Chris Rozee . I've modified all of the files, however. The originals were enlarged beyond their original resolution and many were poorly color balanced, either from the original source or from the scanning process. I have corrected all of the colors and returned the images to what I believe to be their original size.

These are all small JPEG images -- the average is about 3 inches tall and most are about 40K. If your browser doesn't support client-side imagemaps, you'll need to use the links at the bottom.
