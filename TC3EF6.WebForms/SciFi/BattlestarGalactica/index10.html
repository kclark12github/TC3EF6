Battlestar Galactica Images

These are images from a couple of fan magazines from the late seventies, provided by Maryann Panico (PANICO@nsvm.atlantic.edu). I've cleaned them up a bit, but they were all badly scanned.

When you select some of these pictures, you'll also get the original captions. It's awful stuff.

As an added bonus, these pictures come with some fun facts from "Tigerbeat" magazine. Be very afraid.
