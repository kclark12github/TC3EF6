Sikorsky CH-53D/E & HH-53E

The CH-53D/E is operated by the US Navy and Marine Corps as the Sea Stallion/Super Stallion respectively. Another variant, the MH-53E Sea Dragon is specially equiped with minesweeping gear and is used by the Navy in that capacity. The US Air Force operates the HH-53E Pave Low as a special operations platform. All three services operate these aircraft for SAR operations, in fact USAF Cpt. Scott O'Grady was rescued in Bosnia after being shot down by a USMC CH-53.
