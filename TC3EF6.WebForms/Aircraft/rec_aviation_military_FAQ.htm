The latest updates.

(If you can't access the usenet news discussion group rec.aviation.military, ask your Internet provider how to do it.)
You can search the usenet news archive at Google.

The rec.aviation.military FAQ is posted to the usenet newsgroup rec.aviation.military about once per month and is available via WWW, as text files, at
http://www.canit.se/~griffon/aviation/faq/ (primary, updated quicker and more frequently)

If you want to know when the FAQ is updated, there are methods you can use, and for example that's by using one of the several URL-minder services. Note that you should not tell them to watch this page, but the documents of the FAQ themselves.
