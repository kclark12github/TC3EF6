Welcome to

Hello there, regular Alley visitors will be glad to know that I'm back online with a new e-mail address. So please feel free to mail me with all your comments, corrections and suggestions once more. Over the next week you should notice a slightly different Alley build up, there will be incremental updates, mainly bringing present listings up to date and introducing a few new images. Further into the future I'm working on changing the file structure, this will have absolutely no effect upon you but will mean that the Alley can continue to grow, as its present sturcture (or lack of one means great problems arise from very small errors in the HTML). After that I'm thinking about changing the main Alley interface, ie this page. Before I do this I'd like as much comment from you, the users, as possible on what sort of interface you'd like to see. Do you want frames? Would a simplified interface (where the list of links is nowhere near as long) be better or do you like the fact that the present interface allows you to jump to almost any page with the minimum of page loading? The more you tell me about what you'd like the interface to do the better I can design it for you. So please e-mail me-
