F-14  
 

f14-a.wrl - ASCII, 346k
f14-a.zip - zip of f14-a.wrl above, 63k

  • This was originally a 3DS file which you can find in various ftp archives under the name bigf14.3ds (the Mesh Mart and UK VR-SIG have it). I found a reference saying the original dataset comes from Avalon. Done some minor modifications, added the cockpit interior, materials and lighting then converted it to vrml. Yes, I know the missiles are not in the right places - I'll take care of that.

F-15
 

f15-a.wrl - ASCII, 482k
f15-a.zip - zip of f15-a.wrl above, 91k

  • The original data set came as a wireframe performance demo from SGI with an Indigo R4000 I once had (great little workstation - they don't make them like that any more). I used the vertex data and generated the polygons with a little piece of code. Obviously something went wrong around the top side of the air intakes - didn't yet have time to fix it. This one is not yet ready but I still include a link to it for the curious.
