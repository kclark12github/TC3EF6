THE DOLJ COUNTY HALL

Administrates an area of 741300 ha (1,8 mil. acres) with over 760000 inhabitants in the south-west of Romania .

The county has industrial plants manufacturing Diesel electric and electric locomotives, cars, aircrafts, tractors and farming machinery, electrotehnical items, chemical fertilizers, furniture, textile ready mades etc.

Having a strongly developed industry and agriculture Dolj county is an ideal business partner. 

The county hall is directly linked with similar institution from Italy, France, Norway and it is opened to further cooperation with administrative entities from all over the world.
