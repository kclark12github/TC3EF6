About David Davies:

As these histories show I am a small fan of the F-14 Tomcat, but also have an interest in basically all modern military aircraft. As well as writing about F-14's I make 1/72nd scale model kits of them (and of other planes) and am trying to learn the black art of aviation photography.

I would love to hear from anyone who has flown in or worked upon the F-14, especially if they can provide information to improve this page. Also if you have any pictures of the F-14 which you would be willing to trade (in electronic form) it would be great to hear from you. I have a large collection of aircraft pictures from various net sites and a growing collection of my own pictures taken at various UK airshows and airfields.

- David Davies
