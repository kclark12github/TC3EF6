Block 60

These aircraft were in almost every way the same as the XF-14A's, as far as my information goes. BuNos are as follows:-

158612 was assigned to NATC Paxtuent River by Oct 1981. Served with VF-202 as 'AF 206', dates unknown. Presently serves with VF-201 as 'AF 100' and has been upgraded to block 140 standard.
158613 was assigned to NASA in late 1970's, early 1980's, later upgraded to Block 130. Presently in storage at NAS Norfolk.
158614 delivered to Paxtuent River on the 31st August 1972, later upgraded to Block 130, also modified to TARPS configuration. Presently serves with VF-201 as 'AF 120'.
158615 was assigned to PMTC, dates unknown, by 1981 was in storage before delivery to VF-201 in early 1988, later upgraded to Block 130. Seen with VF-14 as 'AC 112' on the 1st August 1995.
158616 was assigned to NATC Paxtuent River by Aug 1973, later upgraded to Block 130, since then has been upgraded to block 140. Seen with VF-201 on the 16th September 1994 and is still with the squadron.
158617 Upgraded to Block 130, later upgraded to Block 140 standard. By November 1995 was in service with VF-201 as 'AF 114', but is no longer with the squadron. Present whereabouts unknown.
158618 was assigned to VX-4, dates unknown, upgraded to Block 130 and then Block 140 standards. By 5th of June 1996 was in service with VF-201 as 'AF 112' and is still in use by them.
158619 Written off on the 22nd of February 1977, circumstances unknown.
