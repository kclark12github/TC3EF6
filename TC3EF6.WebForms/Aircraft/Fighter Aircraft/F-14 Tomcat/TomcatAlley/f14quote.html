Notable Quotables

Question:

"Admiral Connolly, do you think the Navy variant (the F-111B) needs a more powerful engine?" -- Senator John Stennis, former Chairman of the Senate Armed Services Committee.

Answer:

"Mister Chairman, all the thrust in christendom couldn't make a fighter out of that airplane." -- Vice Admiral Thomas F. Connolly, former Deputy Chief of Naval Operations.

"The F-14 and TF series engines represented probably the worst engine/airframe mismatch we have had in many years. TF30 is just a terrible engine and has accounted for 28.2% of all F-14 crashes." -- former Secretary of the Navy John Lehman's testimony before a Congressional Appropriations subcommittee.

"How does one get near it (the F-14)?", MiG-25 pilot Viktor Belenko. He defected with his Foxbat to Japan in 1976.

"F-14 is the world's best total fighter." -- Cdr Mike Denkler, former Skipper of VF-31.

"Let me get my hands on the D-model (the F-14D) and there won't be a fighter on the planet that can stick with me." -- Cdr C.J. 'Heater' Heatley, former Topgun instructor.
