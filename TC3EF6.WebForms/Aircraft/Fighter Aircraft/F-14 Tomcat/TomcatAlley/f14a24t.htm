VF-24 F-14A '200' (BuNo.162608). Taken before VF-24's last cruise this was the CO's aircraft. The Tomcat logo is applied at the NAD Norfolk, when aircraft are repainted after major overhaul and contains information on the finshes used. It's presence here suggests the aircraft has only recently returned from overhaul.
