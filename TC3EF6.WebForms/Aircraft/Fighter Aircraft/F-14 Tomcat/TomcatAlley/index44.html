LEO = Leadership, Experience, Opportunity

Craiova Leo Club is a charitable organisation. It's objectives are to provide humanitarian voluntary services to help young people in general, and in particular those who are 'disadvantaged' in some way. Also, Craiova Leo Club favours aiming its activities at the young of the community with the objective of developing individual qualities as well as a spirit of friendship, help and mutual understanding.
